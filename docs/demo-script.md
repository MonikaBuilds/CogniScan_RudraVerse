# CogniScan Demo Script

## 90-second live demo

### Opening

"Cognitive decline is often noticed too late because early signs are subtle. CogniScan helps identify those warning signals from a short speech-led interaction."

### Screen 1: Executive Overview

- show the value proposition
- mention low-resource deployment and explainable AI

### Screen 2: Screening Studio

- preload a sample patient
- show age, memory score, and caregiver concern
- click `Generate Clinical Summary`

### Screen 3: Results

- point to risk score
- point to risk band
- point to top driver
- explain that the output is interpretable, not black-box

### Screen 4: Cohort Lab

- compare low, moderate, and high-risk synthetic patients
- explain that the model separates profiles meaningfully

### Screen 5: Operations Layer

- show the architecture
- describe this as a speech-first module within a future multimodal platform

### Closing

"Our Round 1 prototype validates the most deployable part of the system today, while setting up a strong path to a full multimodal healthcare screening platform in Round 2."
